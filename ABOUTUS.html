About Us

The Essential Beauty House accentuates on beauty, comfort and stress-free services and packages. The vision is straightforward, everyone deserves an experience that lightens the body and mind.

At Essential Beauty House, we aspire to offer a unique and holistic wellness experience based on the ‘Eat Well; Look Well; Live Well’ principle via our brand. From the refreshing Facial Treatment by Essential Beauty House, to hearty and delicious meals by Evon Lee, we strive to ensure that one gets to experience the best of our expertise.

Burden-Free Spending

Essential Beauty House is the pioneer of single beauty treatments, which does not impose customers with the burden or commitment of a treatment package. By providing a transparent and upfront product, services and cost menu, we provide every valued customer the freedom to choose their treatments each time, and to return to us if they are satisfied with our services.
